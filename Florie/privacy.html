Florie – Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

Florie ("we", "our", or "the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our mobile application.

1. No Account Required

Florie does not require account creation. We do not collect your name, email address, username, or profile picture to use the App.

2. Information We May Collect

2.1 Plant Photos and Camera Access

When you choose to identify a plant, add a plant photo, or run a health check, the App accesses your camera or selected photo only with your permission. Images are used solely to provide plant-related analysis and app features.

2.2 AI Processing

Plant photos may be securely processed by third-party AI services to help identify plants and generate health check insights or care recommendations. Images are not used for advertising purposes.

2.3 Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage data such as:

  • App interactions
  • Feature usage
  • Crash reports
  • Device type and operating system

This information helps us improve performance, reliability, and user experience.

2.4 Notifications and Reminders

If you enable reminders, Florie may use local notification permissions to send plant care alerts such as watering reminders.

2.5 Subscription Information

If you purchase a subscription, payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment details.

3. How We Use Information

Information is used to:

  • Provide plant identification and health check features
  • Store and display your plant collection and care schedules
  • Send reminders you choose to enable
  • Support widgets and app experiences related to plant care tracking
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Manage subscriptions

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

4. Data Storage and Retention

Plant records, photos, care schedules, and health check history may be stored locally on your device. We retain only the minimum information necessary to operate and improve the App.

You may delete the App at any time to remove locally stored data.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party providers for:

  • AI image processing
  • Analytics and crash reporting
  • Subscription management (Apple App Store)

These providers process data according to their own privacy policies.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical safeguards to protect your information. However, no digital system is completely secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Florie is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding access, correction, or deletion of your data. Please contact us for any privacy-related requests.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by changing the "Last updated" date above.
