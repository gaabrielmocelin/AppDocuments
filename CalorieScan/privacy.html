CalorieScan – Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

CalorieScan ("we", "our", or "the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our mobile application.

1. No Account Required

CalorieScan does not require account creation. We do not collect your name, email address, username, or profile picture.

2. Information We May Collect

2.1 Food Photos and Scans

When you choose to scan food, the App accesses your camera or selected photo only with your permission. Images are used solely to analyze food items and estimate nutritional information.

2.2 AI Processing

Food images may be securely processed by third-party AI services to generate calorie and nutritional estimates. Images are not used for advertising purposes.

2.3 Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage data such as:

  • App interactions
  • Feature usage
  • Crash reports
  • Device type and operating system

This information helps us improve performance, reliability, and user experience.

2.4 Subscription Information

If you purchase a subscription, payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment details.

3. How We Use Information

Information is used to:

  • Provide food recognition and nutritional estimates
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Fix bugs and monitor reliability
  • Manage subscriptions

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

4. Data Storage and Retention

Food scan data may be stored locally on your device. We retain only the minimum information necessary to operate and improve the App.

You may delete the App at any time to remove locally stored data.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party providers for:

  • AI image processing
  • Analytics and crash reporting
  • Subscription management (Apple App Store)

These providers process data according to their own privacy policies.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical safeguards to protect your information. However, no digital system is completely secure.

7. Children's Privacy

CalorieScan is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding access, correction, or deletion of your data. Please contact us for any privacy-related requests.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by changing the “Last updated” date above.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact:

Email: support@caloriescan.app
Website: https://caloriescan.app
