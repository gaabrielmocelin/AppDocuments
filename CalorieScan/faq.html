CalorieScan – Frequently Asked Questions

Last updated: February 21, 2026

1. How does CalorieScan work?

CalorieScan uses artificial intelligence to analyze food images and estimate calories and nutritional information. Simply take a photo of your meal, and the app will generate an estimated breakdown.

2. Do I need to create an account?

No. CalorieScan does not require login or account creation. You can use the app immediately after downloading.

3. How accurate are the calorie estimates?

Calorie estimates are generated using AI and may not be perfectly accurate. Results depend on image quality, portion size, and food complexity. The information provided is for informational purposes only.

4. Is CalorieScan medical advice?

No. CalorieScan does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a healthcare professional for dietary or health-related decisions.

5. Does CalorieScan store my photos?

Food images are used only to analyze nutritional information. Scan data may be stored locally on your device. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for more details.

6. How do subscriptions work?

Some features may require a subscription. Payments are processed through Apple’s App Store.

  • Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled.
  • You can manage or cancel your subscription in your Apple ID settings.
  • We do not have access to your payment information.

7. How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel:

  • Open the Settings app on your iPhone
  • Tap your Apple ID at the top
  • Select "Subscriptions"
  • Choose CalorieScan
  • Tap "Cancel Subscription"

8. How can I delete my data?

Since CalorieScan does not require accounts, most data is stored locally. You can delete the app at any time to remove locally stored information.

9. What devices are supported?

CalorieScan is designed for modern iOS devices compatible with the latest supported versions of iOS.

10. How can I contact support?

If you have questions or need assistance, please contact:

Email: support@caloriescan.app
Website: https://caloriescan.app
